Course Summary

Adalo is a no-code application development platform that enables users to design mobile and web applications without the requirement for coding.
.

Course Duration

2 Months.

Prerequisites

Adalo-Powered Mobile App Creation Course is a specially crafted program for children between the ages of 6 and 15. This course will provide students with an introduction to the realm of mobile app development, employing Adalo, a visual app development platform. Classes will convene twice a week, totaling 8 sessions per month.

Prerequisites:

- A computer or tablet with internet connectivity.
- An Adalo account, which can be established free of charge.

Achievements

Upon completing the course, students will accomplish the following:

1. Attain a fundamental comprehension of mobile app development.
2. Gain the competence to design a basic mobile app utilizing Adalo.
3. Learn how to proficiently employ diverse features of Adalo, including buttons, text fields, and images.
4. Develop the ability to navigate and effectively utilize the Adalo interface.
